I am now a PhD candidate in the School of Science and Engineering(SSE), The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen(CUHKSZ), supervised by Prof. Shuguang Cui and Prof. Xiaoguang Han. Previously I obtained my BEng in Software Engineering from Harbin Institute of Technology(HIT), Weihai in 2018.

I am interested in geometric modeling and 3D reconstruction, especially for indoor scene and virtual human.
